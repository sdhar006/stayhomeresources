StayHome includes a resource directory that gives you direct access to trusted information sources to support your need for accurate information about how to maintain physical, financial, and personal wellbeing during the outbreak.

This website has localized resources for all US states and territoies as well as resources that are applicable regardless of where you live. 

The URL of the site is https://uwcirg.github.io/stayhomeresources/ and is also accessible from  tiny.cc/covidlinks or through stayhome.app
